The Duffer's Cup
Participating in the Duffer's Cup are some of the greatest to ever play the game. Each year a ferocious competition occurs on the courses of Central New York culminating in a winner takes all tournament. The winner takes ownership of a beautiful trophy for a year along with the Green Jacket. The trophy is awarded at a lavish banquet at one of the local restaurants. Participants are also eligible for a mid-winter golf excursion to Orland Florida!
